🏆 Winner Story
Hill Birdied the 17th. In a Three-Way Countback, That Was Enough.

David Hill, Tim Plunkett and Zac O'Hehir all finished on nett 64. The back nine could not separate them, all three returning 34. The last six holes eliminated O'Hehir: his nett 24 against Hill and Plunkett's 23. Two left. On the last three holes, Hill shot nett 10 and Plunkett shot nett 12. The difference was Hole 17, a par-3 where Hill made birdie and Plunkett made bogey. Holes 16 and 18 were level between them. The entire two-stroke margin came from one hole. Hill wins.

Hill's card: gross 77 on a par-64 course, nett 64 off a handicap of 13. He went nett 30 on the front and 34 on the back, finishing 10 strokes below the field average of 74.1. Three episodes in, three different winners. The season is not giving anything away.

📈 Form Watch
Ryan: Three Rounds, Three Cuts Made.
Gary Ryan won Episode 1, made the cut in Episode 2 and made the cut again this week with a nett 69 and a twentieth-place finish. The win is on the board. The subsequent rounds have been solid if not spectacular. Three episodes in, he has finished every one of them. That counts for something early in a season.
🎯 Sharpshooter
Bradley. Gross 72. The Lowest Card in the Field.
Mark Bradley plays off a daily handicap of 3. Strip away the handicap strokes and he won the day outright, returning the lowest gross in the 95-player completed field. Gross 72 on a par-64 course, eight over. He finished nineteenth on nett 69, which tells you something about how well the rest of the top eighteen played with their strokes. Bradley did not need most of his.
