Winner Story
Gave a hole away, won by two

Brent Raynsford put up 39 points off 11 in a field of 72 that averaged 28.5, a full 10.5 clear of the pack. There was a pickup at the 6th, that hole handed back to the course entirely, and he still had 2 in hand when the counting stopped.

The card barely leaned either way, 20 out and 19 home. The 12th got him for a double and a single point, and the answer came at the last, a par worth 3 with the stroke. One rough hole, one abandoned, and the rest did the work.

Villain Hole
A point a visit, take it or leave it
The 6th paid out exactly a point a man, 72 points from 72 players, and collected 23 wipes doing it. When the field came back around at the 15th it had barely softened, 1.04 a visit and 25 wipes, the two hardest holes on the card and the same stretch of grass both times. The 3rd took 20 wipes for third place, business as usual.
